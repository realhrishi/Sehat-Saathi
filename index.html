Who are we ?

Bridging Healthcare Gaps Through Artificial Intelligence

Sehat Saathi is an innovative AI-powered chatbot designed to address the critical healthcare information gap in rural and semi-urban India. Our solution leverages WhatsApp and SMS to provide instant, reliable health guidance, myth-busting, and connections to government health services.

The Problem

  • Limited healthcare access in rural areas
  • Health misinformation spreading rapidly
  • Language barriers in health communication
  • Lack of awareness about government health schemes

Our Solution

  • 24/7 AI-powered health guidance
  • Real-time myth-busting capabilities
  • Multi-lingual support for accessibility
  • Direct integration with government APIs
